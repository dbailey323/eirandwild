Fitness Career

I trained to be a fitness instructor when I was 18 and have been helping people on their fitness journeys ever since! I initially trained in group fitness which is why I love classes so much and quickly went on to train in various Les Mills programmes (6 to be exact) where I was also chosen to be a tribe coach, supporting new instructors through their teaching journeys. I also trained in group indoor cycling, Zumba, Zumbatomic, INSANITY and aqua aerobics just to name a few. I completed my Personal training qualification not long after this and have been training clients on a 1:1 basis for well over 10 years now. I love being able to connect with clients on a personal basis whilst also helping them achieve their specific goals. I'll chat during your sessions, but I know how to push those workouts for you!

Pre & Postnatal

Despite being in fitness for so long, I did not specialise in pre and postnatal fitness until having Luna. In 2020, I took the leap and finally did my training course through lockdown. I felt after experiencing pregnancy and postnatal recovery I was better equipped to help my clients, as I have been through some of the same things they will be feeling.

I trained throughout my pregnancy. Throughout the early morning sickness nausea when no one but our close family knew, right through to 36 weeks when I stopped incase I went into labour mid class however I still trained my last PT client 4 days before my due date! I honestly feel keeping my fitness levels up helped me lead a happy and healthy pregnancy along with helping my postnatal recovery after an emergency Caesarean birth.

I am fully qualified to create fitness programmes for both prenatal clients from first trimester to third and postnatal clients juggling new motherhood. I can also give nutritional advice alongside the sessions to help you lead a fit and healthy pre and postnatal journey. You can be rest assured that the sessions are held with the latest medical guidance and are safe for each trimester. Options during sessions will change depending on what trimester you are in, be it first or fourth!

Supporting Motherhood

I completed my baby massage training as I knew I wanted to help support parents and babies to connect and bond even further after my own experiences practising baby massage with Luna. I found how the benefits of massage can help promote sleep in little ones, ease tummy aches and release tension as well as promote bonding with touch and singing. It also wanted to create a safe space to be heard, nurtured and supported postpartum and found this calming space with small groups is the perfect place for this.

I completed my Doula training in 2022 with the Every Birth Matters course. It is something I have wanted to do for a while, but waiting for the right course at the right time was important to me so when this one came along I took the leap and started my training. This included pre coursework, live and online training, post coursework all of which I have passed and am now certified. I am now completing extra training in a mentorship with my course tutor Katy. This means I can gain extra knowledge after the course to further help in your birth, labour and postnatal journey. It also means you get 2 doulas for the price of one as I always have back up on the phone should something unexpected arise, as well as getting a discounted price whilst I complete this extra training. Upon completing my training I also participated in an extra hypnobirthing skills workshop to help assist mums to be and their partners further.

I am however, currently taking a break from this due to navigating our own fertility journey, however my knowledge is always on hand during sessions so ask away! I'm always here to help!
