Strong&Stretched


Mondays @ 7.00pm

Mountsorrel Memorial Centre


Are you ready to create a shaped, sculpted and toned body? All whilst lengthening muscles and releasing the stress on everyday? Helping to prevent injuries and helping you to feel powerful and connected to yourself? Low impact, high burn?

Then STRONG&STRETCHED is here! 30 minutes to blast through some stretch and conditioning exercises where we speed up and hold movements to make your muscles shake and burn followed by a full body stretch to release the tension and hopefully help those next day DOMS! So don’t think it’s going to be easy.. but it will be worth it!
